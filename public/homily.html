About Homily Clock

Character Analysis, Okay, okay. There's absolutely nothing wrong with wanting the best for your family. And while we totally applaud Homily's hard work to ensure a better future for her daughter, we also can't help but point out that her weird fixation on material wealth is a major source of conflict in The Borrowers. As in, half the trouble they get into is all thanks to Mama Clock's penchant for shiny things.
